Empowering Education

In the context of the trend of establishing quality residential schools for promotion of education and ensuring all-round development of tribal students, EMRS Saladahara, Nilgiri, Balasore, Odisha was established in 2020. This boarding school and center of excellence for sports accommodates 90% ST, 10% Non-ST, and PVTG students.
