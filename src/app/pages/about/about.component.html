About EMRS Saladahara

EMRS Saladahar is an initiative of Govt.of lndia,Ministry of Tribal Affairs to impart quality Education and to provide a model platform for mainstreaming of the tribal students .. The school was functional amid the pandemic days of COVID-19 in 2020. The first Batch of students got admitted in June 2020. The same pandemic situation was faced by the 2nd batch of students who were admitted in January 2021. It was a great loss for these two batches of students who lacked accessibility to online learning then. The pt batch of students are going to appear the 10th CBSE BOARD Exam in February 2025 with a optimist view of notable performance.
